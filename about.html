Our Heritage

Our Story

The Orchardix legacy began nearly a century ago in a small valley where the air always smelled of ripening fruit. What started as a family obsession with the perfect apple transformed into a pursuit of artisanal liquid gold.

We merge traditional slow-fermentation methods with modern refinement to ensure every bottle of our cider and mead captures the raw beauty of our land. For us, craft isn't just a label—it's a lifelong commitment to the orchard's soul.

The Orchardix Family

Crafted with
Purpose

At Orchardix, we believe that the best beverages aren't manufactured—they are nurtured. Our philosophy is rooted in respect for the land and the tradition of the craft.

01.

Pure Ingredients

Exclusivity starts with the source. We utilize only fresh-pressed heirloom apples and raw wildflower honey, free from artificial additives or concentrates.

02.

Artisanal Scale

By maintaining small-batch production, we ensure every bottle meets our rigorous standards for complexity, clarity, and character.

03.

Environmental Legacy

Sustainability is our duty. We employ regenerative farming and low-impact brewing techniques to ensure our orchards thrive for generations.

04.

Honest Expression

We don't over-process. Our meads and ciders are a true reflection of the harvest season, celebrating the natural variations of the fruit.
